Workflow, Order Milne Pe

Step 1, Client Brief Lo

Yeh sawaal pucho:

  • Aapka business kya hai? (Brand basics)
  • Audience kaun hai? (Target demographic)
  • Tone kaisa? (Casual / Formal / Funny)
  • Posting frequency? (Daily / Weekly)
  • Hashtags ki preferences?

Step 2, Content Plan Banao

20 posts ka mix:

  • 5 product/service posts
  • 5 educational/tip posts
  • 5 engagement posts (questions, polls)
  • 5 lifestyle/aesthetic posts

Step 3, Claude Se Generate Karo

Master prompt:

Mujhe ek Pakistani [type of business] ke liye Instagram captions chahiye. Brand: [Name] About: [Description] Target: [Audience] Tone: [Tone] 20 captions banao, yeh mix: - 5 product/service highlights - 5 educational tips for audience - 5 engagement posts (questions/polls) - 5 lifestyle/relatable posts Each: 80-120 words. Hashtags include karo (10 per post). Roman Urdu mein casual mix. Format: Day 1: [caption], Day 2: [caption], etc.

Step 4, Edit + Personalize

Claude ka output 80% achi hota hai. Tum 20% edit karte ho, brand voice match karne ke liye.

Step 5, Deliver

Google Doc mein paste karke share karo. Ya Notion. Client ko schedule karna easy ho.
