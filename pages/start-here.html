Pehle, Honest Baat

Online paise kamana easy nahi hai. Lekin AI ki wajah se bohot easier ho gaya hai. Pehle 6 mahine lagte the kuch banane mein, ab 1 hafta. Pehle coding seekhni padti thi ab voice se kaam ho jaata hai.

Lekin "easier" ka matlab "automatic" nahi hai. Tumhe khud kaam karna hoga. Daily 1-2 ghante minimum. Patience chahiye. Aur sabse important, start karna padega.

Sach yeh hai: 90% log start hi nahi karte. Yahi tumhara biggest advantage hai. Agar tum sirf shuru kar do, tum already top 10% mein ho.
