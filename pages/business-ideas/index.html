🎯 Mera Suggestion: Yahan Se Shuru Karo

Beginner ke liye easiest 3 paths:

  1. Instagram improvement, kisi local business ka. Content writing service.
  2. QR Menu, restaurants ke liye. Simple HTML page, hosting free.
  3. Tuition center app, attendance + fees. Recurring revenue.

Ek pe focus karo, master karo, phir scale.

Pehla Client Kaise Pao?

  1. Family/Friends: Sabse pehla client wahin se aata hai. Free ya cheap pe karo, case study banao.
  2. Mohalle ke shops: Walk in karo, pitch karo. Local touch zyaada trust banata hai.
  3. Instagram DM: Local brands ko DM karo with sample.
  4. Facebook groups: Pakistani business groups join karo.
  5. Cousin's wedding: Sab vendors meet karo, business cards do.
