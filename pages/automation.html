Automation Services

Yeh ThemeKnock ka asli kaam hai. Businesses ke chhote tasks ko automate karo. Setup ek baar, paise har month. Yeh path slow start hota hai, lekin compound growth banta hai.

Automation Kya Hai?

Har business mein bohot saare repetitive kaam hote hain, emails, data entry, reports, social media posting, order tracking. Automation ka matlab, yeh kaam scripts/AI se khud-ba-khud ho jaata hai.

Tum business ke liye yeh systems banao. Setup ek baar. Phir unhe maintenance fee charge karte raho.

Real Examples, Maine Yeh Banayi Hain

📅
Salon Booking System

Problem: Salon roz 50+ phone calls, appointments book karne ke liye. Receptionist ka time waste.

Solution: Online booking page. Customer slot select karta hai. Automatic confirmation SMS. Calendar sync.

Pricing: Setup $300, monthly maintenance $50.

📧
Restaurant Email Auto-Reply

Problem: Restaurant 100+ emails roz, bookings, queries. Owner manage nahi kar sakta.

Solution: AI email auto-reply system. Common questions ka auto-jawab. Important emails owner ko forward.

Pricing: Setup $400, monthly $80.

📱
Social Media Auto-Post

Problem: Brand ko daily 3 posts chahiye, staff hire karne se costly.

Solution: AI content generator + scheduler. 30 din ka content ek baar. Auto-post Instagram + Facebook.

Pricing: Setup $500, monthly $150 (content + scheduling).

🛒
Ecommerce Order Tracker

Problem: Small Pakistani ecommerce, orders Excel mein, tracking manual.

Solution: Custom dashboard. Auto-organize orders. Customer ko delivery updates SMS pe.

Pricing: Setup $600, monthly $100.

Tools You Will Use

  • Claude, code likhne ke liye, logic samjhne ke liye
  • n8n, visual automation builder (free, self-hosted)
  • Make.com, drag-drop automation (paid)
  • Zapier, simplest automation tool (paid)
  • Airtable, spreadsheet + database
  • Supabase, backend database (free tier)
  • GitHub Pages, hosting (free, simplest)

Don't worry, koi bhi pehle se nahi aata. Sab Claude se sikho ge.

Income Math

Realistic 1-year plan:

  • Mahina 1-3: 1 client setup (learning), $300-500 one-time
  • Mahina 4-6: 3 clients setup + monthly maintenance = $1,500-3,000/mo
  • Mahina 7-12: 6-8 clients = $3,000-6,000/mo
  • Year 2: 15+ clients = $5,000-10,000/mo

Compound effect: Har naye client ke saath income bdthi hai. Old clients leave nahi karte easily, kyunki system unke business ka part ban jata hai.

Pehla Client Kaise Pao?

1

Apne Network Mein Dhundo

Family, friends, dosto ki shops, parents ke business contacts. Pehla client wahin se. Free ya cheap pe karo, case study banao.

2

LinkedIn Pe Active Raho

Pakistani business owners ko follow karo. Comment karo. Helpful posts likho. Slowly trust build hoti hai.

3

Cold Outreach

Local businesses identify karo jinhe automation se faida hoga. Email/WhatsApp pe pitch karo:

Hi [Business Name], Aapke business ka [observation about their pain point, e.g., "phone constantly busy" / "manual order tracking"] dekha. Main businesses ke liye AI automation systems banata hoon. Yahi exact problem ko maine [previous client] ke liye solve kiya, result: [time saved or money saved]. Aapke liye 15-min free consultation karunga, agar pasand aaye toh discuss karenge. No obligation. Available kal subah ya shaam?
4

Free Demo Dikhao

Pitch ke baad, ek small demo banake do. Live dikhao. Trust banta hai instantly.

Workflow, Project Karne Ka Tarika

  1. Discovery Call (30 min), client ki problem samjho. Process map karo.
  2. Proposal Likho, Claude se. What you'll build, timeline, pricing.
  3. Contract Signed, 50% advance. Always.
  4. Build (1-2 weeks), Claude + tools. Daily updates client ko.
  5. Test + Train, client ko sikhao kaise use karna hai.
  6. Launch, final 50% payment. Done.
  7. Monthly Maintenance, recurring fee for support.

Yeh Path Kis Ke Liye Hai?

  • Tum technical sense rakhte ho, coding nahi aati lekin samajh aati hai
  • Long-term thinking, pehle 3 mahine slow OK
  • Client communication confident
  • Detail-oriented, chhoti cheezein matter karti hain

Honest baat: Yeh sabse mushkil path hai start karne ke liye. Lekin sabse zyada paise yahan hain. Agar tum FSc/BSc student ho aur 6 mahine commit kar sakte ho, yeh tumhari zindagi badal sakti hai.
