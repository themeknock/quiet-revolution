Fiverr Guide, Pehla Order Tak

Fiverr Pakistani freelancers ke liye sabse easy start hai. AI ki wajah se gigs aur powerful ho gayi hain. Yeh complete walkthrough hai, account banane se pehla order tak.

Fiverr Kya Hai?

Fiverr ek global marketplace hai jahan log "gigs" beechte hain, yani specific services jaise logo design, content writing, video editing. Pakistani sellers top 10 mein hain globally.

  • Foreign clients dollars mein pay karte hain
  • Free se shuru kar sakte ho, koi joining fee nahi
  • AI tools ne kaam aur fast kar diya, same time mein zyada orders
  • Pakistani bank pe withdrawal, Payoneer ya bank transfer

Account Setup, Step by Step

1

Sign Up Karo

fiverr.com pe jao → "Become a Seller" pe click. Email se ya Google se sign up.

  • Username choose karo: simple aur professional. Example: talha_aiwriter
  • Pakistan select karo as country
  • Phone verify karo
2

Profile Banao, Sabse Important

Yeh wo first impression hai jo client dekhega. Yeh karo:

  • Photo: Apni clear picture, formal but friendly. Selfie nahi.
  • Tagline: "AI-Powered Copywriter Helping Brands Grow", short, clear.
  • Description (300 words): Tum kya karte ho, kis ke liye, kya benefit. Use Claude to write this!
  • Skills: 5-10 add karo: AI writing, ChatGPT, copywriting, etc.
  • Languages: English (Fluent), Urdu (Native)

Pro tip: Profile description likhne ke liye, Claude ko bolo:

Mera Fiverr profile description likho. Main AI-powered copywriter hoon Pakistan se. 2 saal experience. SaaS aur ecommerce brands ke liye kaam karta hoon. Tone: confident, friendly. 300 words. Bullet points include karo. Buyer ka problem aur mera solution highlight karo.
3

Pehli Gig Banao

Ek niche pe focus karo. Mera suggestion: AI Copywriting, sabse demand hai.

Gig title:

I will write SEO-optimized AI copywriting for your website or business

3 Pricing Packages:

  • Basic ($10): 300 words, 1 revision, 2 din delivery
  • Standard ($25): 800 words + SEO keywords, 3 revisions, 2 din
  • Premium ($50): 1500 words + SEO + meta description, unlimited revisions, 1 din
4

Gig Description Likho

Claude se likhwao, yeh prompt:

Mera Fiverr gig hai "AI copywriting". Description likho, 1200 characters. Structure: hook, problem, solution, what you get, why me. Bullet points use karo. Friendly aur confident tone. End mein call-to-action.
5

Gig Images Banao

3 images chahiye, minimum. Yeh tools use karo (free):

  • Canva: templates ready, customize karo
  • Google Flow / Midjourney: custom AI images
  • Figma: agar design aati hai

Image specs: 1280x769 px. Bright colors. Bold text overlay. "AI-Powered" highlight karo.

6

FAQs + Tags Add Karo

FAQs mein yeh add karo:

  • "Do you use AI for writing?", Haan, AI tools use karta hoon for efficiency, lekin manual review aur quality check zaroor karta hoon.
  • "How fast is delivery?", Basic: 2 days. Premium: 1 day.
  • "Revisions?", Standard 3, Premium unlimited.

Tags (search terms): ai writing, copywriting, content, seo, blog post, website copy

Pehla Order Kaise Pao?

Hafta 1, Patience

Fiverr ka algorithm naye sellers ko rank karne mein 7-14 din leta hai. Patience.

Hafta 2, Buyer Requests Use Karo

Fiverr pe "Buyer Requests" section hota hai jahan buyers post karte hain, "mujhe yeh chahiye". Tum daily 10 requests pe apply karo. Yahi pehla order milne ka raasta hai.

Apply karte waqt:

  • Personalized message likho, copy paste nahi
  • Buyer ka name use karo
  • Apni gig ka link add karo
  • Realistic price aur timeline do

Hafta 3-4, Reviews Banao

Pehla order, even $5 ka, accept karo. Quality kaam dao. 5-star review zaroori hai.

Pehle 3 reviews ke baad, orders apne aap aane lagte hain.

AI-Powered Workflow, Order Aane Pe

  1. Buyer ka brief padho carefully
  2. Wispr daboo, Claude ko bolo: "Yeh brief hai [paste karo]. 800 words ka SEO blog likho is topic pe, keywords: [list]. Tone professional. Audience: [audience]."
  3. Claude jawab dega, review karo
  4. 2-3 iterations: "Pehla paragraph short karo", "Examples add karo"
  5. Final version client ko bhejo
  6. Same brief ka kaam pehle 3 ghante leta tha. Ab 30 minute.

Withdrawal, Pakistan Mein Paise Kaise Lao?

  1. Payoneer Account: payoneer.com pe sign up. Free hai.
  2. Fiverr → Payoneer link karo: Settings → Billing → Add Payoneer
  3. Payoneer → Pakistan bank: Apna Pakistani bank account add karo Payoneer pe
  4. Earnings transfer: Fiverr → Payoneer (instant) → Pak bank (1-3 days)

Fee: ~3% total. Standard hai.

Common Mistakes, Yeh Mat Karna

  • Multiple gigs ek saath, pehle ek gig pe master ban jao
  • Bohot kam pricing, $5 mat lagao, $10 minimum. Race to bottom mein nahi jaana.
  • Slow response, buyers 1-2 ghante mein response chahte hain. Notification on rakho.
  • Bad English, Claude se proofread karwao. Always.
  • Direct AI copy paste, review karo, personalize karo. Buyers detect kar lete hain.
