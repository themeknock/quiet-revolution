Claude Ko Effectively Use Kaise Karen?

Claude tumhare prompt ki quality jitna achi, utna hi acha jawab. Yeh teen rules follow karo:

Rule 1, Context Do

Sirf "blog likho" mat bolo. Yeh batao:

  • Topic kya hai
  • Audience kaun hai
  • Tone kaisa chahiye (formal/casual)
  • Length kitni
Pakistani salon ke liye Instagram caption likho. Audience: 18-35 saal ki larkiyaan jo facials karwati hain. Tone: friendly, energetic. 4 lines, hashtags ke saath. Roman Urdu mein.

Rule 2, Examples Do

Agar specific style chahiye, example dikhao Claude ko:

Mujhe LinkedIn posts likhne hain. Yeh mera pehla post tha jo viral gaya, [post paste karo]. Iska tone aur style follow karke 5 aur posts likho, software engineering pe.

Rule 3, Iterate Karo

Pehla output perfect nahi hota. Bas keh do, "isse better banao" ya "yeh part change karo":

  • "Pehla paragraph short karo"
  • "Tone professional kar do"
  • "Keywords add karo: AI, automation, Pakistan"

Claude Mobile App

Phone pe chahiye? Claude.ai mobile pe bhi chalti hai, browser se. Ya official app download karo:

  • iPhone, App Store
  • Android, Play Store

Mobile pe voice input bhi available hai, bolne se prompt ban jaata hai.

Common Mistakes, Yeh Mat Karna

  • Sirf chhote prompts likhna, "blog likho", koi context nahi. Result bekar.
  • Pehle output pe stop kar dena, refine karte raho. Iterate karo.
  • Hindi/Urdu script mein likhna, Roman Urdu use karo. Better samajhti hai.
  • Sirf English assume karna, Claude Roman Urdu, Hindi, Punjabi, sab samajhti hai.
  • Privacy data daalna, passwords, OTPs, yeh kabhi nahi.
