## 🎨 DESIGN PRINCIPLES

- **Light theme**, easy reading on mobile, projector-friendly
- **High contrast**, pakistani students often have older phones
- **Roman Urdu first**, students think in Roman Urdu
- **Mobile-first**, most will access on phones
- **Fast**, no heavy frameworks, vanilla HTML/CSS/JS
- **Premium feel**, looks like a paid course resource
- **Same green accent**, consistent with talk slides
